About Andris

Based in Veresegyház, Hungary, Andris explores the delicate balance between light and shadow, tradition and innovation. Each piece is a meditation on color, texture, and the stories that emerge when paint meets canvas.

Working primarily with oils, the focus remains on capturing fleeting moments and emotions, translating them into visual narratives that invite contemplation and connection.

The practice is rooted in a deep respect for classical techniques while embracing contemporary expression, creating works that exist at the intersection of timelessness and modernity.
